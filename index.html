ORIGIN

Coconut rice is typically prepared by soaking white rice in coconut milk or cooking it with coconut flakes.

coconut rice is found in many cultures of the world, it is believed to have its origins in the South Asia, Southeast Asia and Latin America regions.

Below are the steps to prepare coconut rice

  1. Take a pan and heat oil in it. When it is hot and not smoking, add mustard seeds.
  2. Add urad dal and channa dal.
  3. Add roasted peanuts and saute till it becomes golden brown.
  4. Add green chilli, dry red chilli and curry leaves. Saute for a minute. Add asafoetida and salt.
  5. Add grated coconut and fry for few minutes. Make sure the coconut is moist while doing it.
  6. Reduce the flame to low or turn off the stove. Add rice. Mix well till the rice gets warm.
  7. Garnish with coriander leaves and serve hot.
